FAQ

What do I need to do to start using the LFL-E-Payment Ticketing

For you to purchase via e-Payment, you have to register as a LFL member. Please refer to the link below for further details and guidance.

Can I select my own seats throuth the LFL E-Payment Ticketing/Mobile App?

Yes, you can select any available seats through the LFL E-Payment Ticketing / Mobile App

How many tickets can i purchase through LFL-E-Payment Ticketing/Mobile Phone

The maximum tickets per transaction is 12 via GCS E-Payment ticketing and 6 via Mobile App.

IF I do not receive the Confirmation ID status page from LFL-E-Payment after payment where can I check my transaction history?

Please ensure that you are logged in. Kindly go the E-Payment Ticketing page again and you will be able to view your Booking History. Alternatively, you can check your email for the SelfPrint Ticket with the subject GSC e-Payment Ticketing: Ticket Purchase Status. Direct link to E-Payment Ticketing

How do i retrieve my transaction history if i did not receive the Confirmation Barcode in Mobile APP after the succesful payment

Please check your email for the SelfPrint Ticket with subject LFL e-Payment Ticketing: Ticket Purchase Status. If you are a LFL Member, you may refer to the History page and click on the “Refresh” button. Alternatively, please contact our Customer Relations at 012- 345 6000 (Monday - Friday from 9am to 6pm excluding Public Holidays) or email to contact@ljlcinema.com for assistance.

What should I do if received an Unsuccessful message after the payment?

Check if your card / account was charged. If no, please proceed to repurchase the tickets. If yes, please check your transaction history page or check your email for the SelfPrint Ticket. Alternatively, please contact our Customer Relations at 012- 345 6000 (Monday to Friday from 9am to 6pm excluding Public Holidays) or email to contact@ljlcinema.com for assistance.
